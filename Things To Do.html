Things To Do

Explore the many attractions

Explore the many attractions at Gardens by the Bay by purchasing tickets to enter places such as Cloud Forest, Flower Dome, Floral Fantasy, Supertree Observatory and OCBC Skyway! Purchases can be made at their website at https://ticket.gardensbythebay.com.sg/product/listing

Picnic By the Bay

Enjoy a special picnic experience at the Gardens amidst our picturesque spots with a start-to-finish service; from a personal buggy ride to your picnic spot, to a welcome drink to relax with while food is being freshly prepared, all you have to do is show up!

This Picnic by the Bay Outdoor Gardens experience could be yours!

Dine and Shop

Treat yourself to an idyllic day, dining in the lush embrace of Nature. Satisfy your palate with our wide array of gastronomic options, spanning from gourmet dining to popular hawker fare. After meal, take an easy walk to our gift shop. Browse our delightful display of merchandise curated just for nature lovers.

Christmas Wonderland

An annual event held at the end of the year, Christmas Wonderland provides everyone a snowy dreamland in a tropical country where they get to experience carnival games, festive lights, a Christmas parade, festive entertainment, a garden rhapsody and so much more!
